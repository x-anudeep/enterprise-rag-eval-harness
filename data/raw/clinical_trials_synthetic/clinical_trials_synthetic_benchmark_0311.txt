Clinical trial protocol 0311: Adverse Event Reporting

This synthetic benchmark document describes adverse event reporting requirements for the clinical trials compliance program. Teams must maintain institutional review board review evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects adverse event reporting with participant withdrawal so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a annual cadence, retained for at least 41 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every adverse event reporting activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
