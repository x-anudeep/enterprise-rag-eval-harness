Clinical trial protocol 0122: Data Monitoring Committee

This synthetic benchmark document describes data monitoring committee requirements for the clinical trials compliance program. Teams must maintain documented medical assessment evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects data monitoring committee with source data verification so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a semiannual cadence, retained for at least 32 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every data monitoring committee activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
