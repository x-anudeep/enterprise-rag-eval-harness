Clinical trial protocol 0150: Informed Consent

This synthetic benchmark document describes informed consent requirements for the clinical trials compliance program. Teams must maintain sponsor notification evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects informed consent with protocol deviations so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a semiannual cadence, retained for at least 60 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every informed consent activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
