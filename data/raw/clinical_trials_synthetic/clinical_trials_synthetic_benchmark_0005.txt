Clinical trial protocol 0005: Source Data Verification

This synthetic benchmark document describes source data verification requirements for the clinical trials compliance program. Teams must maintain sponsor notification evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects source data verification with randomization controls so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a quarterly cadence, retained for at least 35 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every source data verification activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
