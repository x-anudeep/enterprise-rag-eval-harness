FDA quality guidance 0328: Data Integrity

This synthetic benchmark document describes data integrity requirements for the fda compliance program. Teams must maintain change control approval evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects data integrity with supplier qualification so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a monthly cadence, retained for at least 58 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every data integrity activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
