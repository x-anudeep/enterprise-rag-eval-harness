This synthetic benchmark document describes audit trail review requirements for the fda compliance program. Teams must maintain inspection ready evidence evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

Records should be reviewed on a monthly cadence, retained for at least 54 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.
