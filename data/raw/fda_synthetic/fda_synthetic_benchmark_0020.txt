FDA quality guidance 0020: Software Validation

This synthetic benchmark document describes software validation requirements for the fda compliance program. Teams must maintain risk based verification evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects software validation with electronic records so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a monthly cadence, retained for at least 50 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every software validation activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
