FDA quality guidance 0101: Supplier Qualification

This synthetic benchmark document describes supplier qualification requirements for the fda compliance program. Teams must maintain traceable requirements evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects supplier qualification with audit trail review so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a quarterly cadence, retained for at least 71 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every supplier qualification activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
