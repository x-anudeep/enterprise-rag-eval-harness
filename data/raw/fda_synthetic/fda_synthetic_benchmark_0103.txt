FDA quality guidance 0103: Electronic Records

This synthetic benchmark document describes electronic records requirements for the fda compliance program. Teams must maintain change control approval evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects electronic records with complaint handling so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a annual cadence, retained for at least 73 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every electronic records activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
