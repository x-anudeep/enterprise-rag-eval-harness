HIPAA operating procedure 0001: Audit Controls

This synthetic benchmark document describes audit controls requirements for the hipaa compliance program. Teams must maintain quarterly access review evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects audit controls with risk analysis so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a quarterly cadence, retained for at least 31 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every audit controls activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
