HIPAA operating procedure 0180: Minimum Necessary Access

This synthetic benchmark document describes minimum necessary access requirements for the hipaa compliance program. Teams must maintain privacy officer approval evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects minimum necessary access with breach notification so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a monthly cadence, retained for at least 30 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every minimum necessary access activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
