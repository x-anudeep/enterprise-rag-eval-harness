HIPAA operating procedure 0224: Risk Analysis

This synthetic benchmark document describes risk analysis requirements for the hipaa compliance program. Teams must maintain incident response escalation evidence and update the control owner when exceptions affect patient safety, privacy, quality, or trial data integrity.

The procedure connects risk analysis with device disposal so retrieval tests can verify both keyword matching and semantic matching. Records should be reviewed on a monthly cadence, retained for at least 74 months, and made available for audit, inspection, or sponsor oversight when requested.

Responsible teams should document the rationale, source system, reviewer, due date, and closure evidence for every risk analysis activity. Escalation is required when the activity is overdue, incomplete, unsupported by evidence, or inconsistent with the approved compliance procedure.
