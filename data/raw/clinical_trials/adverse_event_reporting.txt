Clinical trial teams must collect, assess, and report adverse events according to the protocol,
investigator brochure, and applicable regulations. Serious adverse events require expedited
review when they result in death, are life-threatening, require hospitalization, cause
persistent disability, or represent an important medical event.

Investigators should evaluate severity, causality, expectedness, and outcome for each event.
Sponsors must maintain safety oversight, reconcile safety data, and notify regulators and
ethics committees when reporting criteria are met.
